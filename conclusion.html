Conclusion & Future Work

In summary, this project aims to provide insights into panic attacks, their triggers, and potential lifestyle factors that may influence severity. Once all visualizations are complete, we expect to draw 2-3 key conclusions that can help guide treatment and prevention strategies.

Future work may include deeper analysis of co-occurring mental health conditions, longitudinal studies on therapy/medication effectiveness, and the integration of real-time monitoring data for more accurate predictions.
